Features

Everything your kitchen needs

From tracking what's in your fridge to generating your shopping list automatically — PantryPal handles it all.

Pantry Inventory
Track every item in your kitchen. Toggle items between in-stock and out-of-stock with a single tap. Add categories, quantities, and units.
Auto Shopping List
Your shopping list builds itself. Every out-of-stock item automatically appears in My List — ready when you head to the store.
Expiry Reminders
Set expiry dates on items and get notified before they go bad. No more surprises at the back of the fridge.
Multiple Lists
Create separate lists for your weekly shop, the bulk store, or a dinner party. Keep things organised without the clutter.
Fast-Add Templates
Save bundles like "Weekly Basics" or "BBQ Supplies" and add everything to your list in one tap. Your future self will thank you.
Smart Refill
PantryPal learns your habits. Smart Refill predicts what you'll likely need based on your purchase patterns and suggests items before you run out.
How it works

Three steps to a stress-free kitchen

Getting started takes less than a minute. No sign-up, no sync setup, no onboarding maze.

1
Add your pantry items
Type in what you keep at home — milk, eggs, pasta, olive oil. Assign categories and quantities to stay organised.
2
Mark things out of stock
When you finish something, tap it out of stock. PantryPal instantly moves it to your shopping list. No double entry.
3
Shop and check off
Open My List at the store, tap each item as you drop it in the basket. It disappears from your list and goes back in stock automatically.
4
Let Smart Refill do the rest
Over time PantryPal learns what you buy and when, surfacing suggestions before you even realise you need them.
